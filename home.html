ABOUT US

  • The project titled “Ontology- based mobile application for Inorganic Chemistry” will be developed based on G.C.E Advanced Level Inorganic Chemistry.
  • Through this project user will be able to
  • identify a chemical element or chemical compound and bonds in chemical elements or chemical compounds ,
  • identify end products of a chemical reaction,
  • balance a chemical equation and it will generate questions based on Inorganic Chemistry for the user.
  • Identifying Chemical elements and Bonds and Identifying End Products of a Reaction will be creatively represented by 3D models.

Introduction

The main Target of this project is developing an android based mobile application to comprehend chemical bonds, end products of a chemical reaction and generating questions based on Inorganic Chemistry.
